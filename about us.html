ABOUT US

OUR STORY

Born from a deep love for authentic Nigerian cuisine, Ile Iyan Reloaded began as a dream to bring the vibrant flavours and rich culinary heritage of our homeland to your table. We started with a simple belief: food should not only nourish the body but also tell a story, evoke memories, and connect communities. From humble beginnings, we've grown, but our commitment to traditional recipes, fresh ingredients, and exceptional service remains unwavering. Every dish we serve is a tribute to the roots that inspire us, prepared with passion and a touch of reload that makes it uniquely ours. We invite you to be part of our story, one delicious meal at a time.

MISSION

To deliver an unparalleled Nigerian dining experience through authentic flavors, warm hospitality, and a vibrant atmosphere.

VISION

To be the leading destination for Nigerian cuisine, celebrated for our commitment to quality, community, and culinary innovation.

WHAT WE OFFER

A diverse menu of traditional and contemporary Nigerian dishes, catering, private events, and a welcoming space for all.

DISCOVER OUR DELICIOUS MENU

At Ile Iyan Reloaded, our menu is a celebration of Nigeria's rich food culture. From quick, satisfying LITE menu perfect for a casual snack, to our hearty MAIN COURSES that showcase the depth of our culinary heritage, and our generously portioned PLATTERS ideal for sharing with friends and family, there's something to tantalize every palate. Each dish is prepared with the freshest local ingredients, ensuring an authentic and unforgettable taste experience.
